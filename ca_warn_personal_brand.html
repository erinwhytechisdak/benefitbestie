2026 Sector Forecast
🏭
Manufacturing
Continued Decline
AI-driven automation permanently eliminating middle-skill production roles. Boeing alone filed 398 notices since tracking began. Food processing, aerospace, and semiconductor manufacturing all contracting — structural, not cyclical.
🏥
Healthcare
Accelerating Risk
$30B/year in Medi-Cal cuts from H.R. 1. Blue Shield CA cut 400+ jobs in 2025. 446 hospitals at closure risk nationally. Covered California premiums up 10.3% for 2026. Directly impacts employer plan design and benefit strategy.
🚚
Transportation
High Tariff Exposure
Container volumes from China slowed sharply post-April 2025 tariffs. Freight recession now in year 3. Mid-2026 further softening likely as tariff impact propagates through supply chains.
🛍️
Retail
Emerging Pressure
Surged to top industry in Jan 2026. Inventory shortages, declining consumer confidence, and tariff-driven cost increases will pressure margins into H2 2026. Watch Q2–Q3 for acceleration.
💻
Tech
Elevated, Stabilizing
127,000 US tech workers laid off in 2025 — 3rd straight elevated year. AI-for-headcount substitution now explicit strategy across Oracle, Amazon, Salesforce. Sustained displacement, not spikes.
📋
Overall 2026
Fragile Market
2025 US job cuts totaled ~1.2M — up 54% YoY, highest outside COVID/GFC. Virtually no net job creation since March 2025. 2026 expected to mirror 2025 patterns per leading economists.

Compliance & Policy Context

Cal-WARN Act — Key Thresholds & 2026 Updates (SB 617)

75+ employees — covered employer threshold
50+ workers in any 30-day period = mass layoff
60 days advance written notice required
Any closure — regardless of size, is covered
100-mile relocation — triggers notice obligation
New Jan 1, 2026 — SB 617:
Must disclose workforce services coordination plan
Local Workforce Development Board contact required
CalFresh food assistance info mandatory in notice
Functioning employer email & phone now required
Service coordination within 30 days of notice
